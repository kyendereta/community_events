What We Do

Fun Friday is an event brought to you by Podii every two weeks at 1630 - 1730hrs EAT

  • To provide a platform for participation and sharing of knowledge and different experiences depending on the topic of discussion.
  • To provide a good opportunity for sharing and discussing new ideas that are not only tech related but also all-round.
  • To provide participants the convenience of attending a relaxing session from the comfort of their offices, homes, school e.t.cwhere they are free to relieve themselves of the week's hustle.
  • To give platform that will help in networking with a variety of people with different interests.
  • To provide a platform to unleash research on new technologies and other findings.
